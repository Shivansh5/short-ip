Why alternative IP formats?

Security testing

Alternate encodings can bypass naive IP-based filters or WAF rules during authorized pentests.

Network tools

Many system utilities and APIs accept decimal, octal, or hex IPs natively (ping, curl, browsers).

Education

Understand how IPv4 addresses are stored as 32-bit integers and represented in different bases.

CTF challenges

Capture-the-flag competitions often encode IPs in unusual formats as part of puzzles.
